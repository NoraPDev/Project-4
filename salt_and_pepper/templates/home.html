Tom yum kung

Also called tom yum kung, this hot and sour shrimp soup is generally touted as the dish that defines Thai cuisine. It's derived from the Thai words “tom,” which refers to the boiling process, “yam,” a Thai spicy and sour salad, and “kung” for shrimp.
